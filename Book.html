My Favourite Book

Hunger Games: Mocking Jay

This book changed my life because I found it at the right time in my youth.
Although looking back I do not find this to be a a good book, the characters and scenarios felt alive just because I willed them to be.
Even though I have read many captivating stories since then, this holds a special place in my heart just because it influenced me.
This was the first time that I had followed a character for multiple books and watching her grow made the world feel very important and sucked me right in.
